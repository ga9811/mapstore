FAQ

Common Questions

How do I order a custom map? +

Browse our product range on the Products page and contact us to place an order. Our online ordering system is currently launching — contact us directly and we'll walk you through the process.

What wood types are available? +

We currently offer Birch and Red Oak plywood, sourced from Winsor Plywood in Winnipeg. Birch gives high-contrast engravings; Red Oak offers warm, classic tones.

What sizes do you offer? +

Maps are available in Small (17×12 inches), Medium (21×15 inches), and Large (30×21 inches) for both Birch and Red Oak.

How accurate is the preview? +

Very accurate. Our beta trial showed customers consistently impressed by how closely the finished map matched the on-screen 3D render.

Do you ship internationally? +

Currently we serve Canada primarily, with US shipping available. Contact us to discuss international shipping options.

What's the turnaround time? +

Typically 5–10 business days from order confirmation to delivery. Rush orders may be available — contact us to discuss.
