Our Story

Built on a Love
for Maps & Craft

Map-Art Store was founded with a single mission: to make personalised, high-quality laser-engraved maps accessible to everyone — powered by technology that lets you see your vision before it's made.

Mission & Vision

More Than Décor —
It's a Connection

Maps carry memory. The neighbourhood where you grew up, the city where you fell in love, the cabin on the lake your family visits every summer. We believe those places deserve to be celebrated and displayed.

Our technology allows buyers to move from concept to a custom, real-time rendered product in minutes — a self-serve experience that feels closer to Amazon than to the traditional custom-craft marketplace.

"To bridge distances by encapsulating memories through intricately designed, personalised geographical engravings, fostering a connection between people and places."
— Map-Art Store Mission Statement

Our Values

What We Stand For

🎨

Craftsmanship First

Every map is a product of precision engineering and genuine craft. We never compromise on quality of materials or the accuracy of our laser-cutting process.

⚡

Innovation in UX

We believe the custom product experience should be fast, intuitive, and satisfying — not slow and cumbersome. Real-time previews change everything.

🇨🇦

Proud to Be Canadian

Our team, our IP, our manufacturing, and our hearts are in Winnipeg. Supporting local suppliers and creating Canadian jobs matters to us deeply.

🌿

Sustainability Matters

We source materials locally to reduce our carbon footprint and explore sustainable, eco-friendly material options as we grow our product range.

Our Journey

The Map-Art Store Timeline

Jan 2021

Project Kicks Off

Technology development begins with Norima Consulting. First UI prototypes and API architecture designed.

Jul 2022

Beta Trial Complete

20 custom maps ordered and manufactured. Valuable customer feedback collected across 16 beta users.

Feb 2023

Beta Report Published

Comprehensive Final Project Report and Beta Trial Assessment published. Updated software designs delivered.

Nov 2023

Business Plan Finalised

Full commercial business plan completed. Seeking $126,000 in funding to accelerate the 2024 launch.

Q1 2024

Commercial Launch

MVP product launches in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Recruitment of third co-founder underway.

2025–2028

National & International Expansion

Scale across Canada and into the United States. Expand product range to five categories. Target $2.2M revenue by Year 5.
