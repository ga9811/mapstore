We're a small team with big ambitions. Based in Winnipeg, Manitoba, we're looking for passionate people to join us as we bring personalised laser-engraved maps to homes and offices across Canada and beyond.

Why Map-Art Store?

Small Team.
Big Impact.

At Map-Art Store, you won't be a cog in a machine. You'll be a core part of building something genuinely new — a company that combines technology, craftsmanship, and personalisation to create products that people treasure.

We're headquartered in Winnipeg, Manitoba, and we're proud to be building a Canadian business, creating Canadian jobs, and contributing to the local economy. We've already invested $200,000 into Manitoba, and we're just getting started.
