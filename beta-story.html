Beta Trial · 2022

How We Proved
the Concept

In 2022, we ran a real-world beta trial with 20 custom maps ordered through our platform by 16 participants. Here's what we learned — and how it shaped everything that came after.

The Trial

20 Maps. Real Customers.
Real Feedback.

Our beta trial was designed by Norima Consulting to assess the performance of our preview generation technology and order processing system. Each participant ordered a custom map for just $1 — a real transaction through our Shopify store.

They received their laser-cut maps, assembled them, and shared detailed feedback on the website experience, product quality, and assembly process. The results were overwhelmingly positive — and illuminated exactly what to improve next.

Lessons Learned

Every Map Made Us Better

💪

Product Quality

Maps were highly accurate and matched the on-screen preview. Customers were consistently impressed with the laser-cut detail.

⏱️

Rendering Speed

Average render time of 14 seconds was higher than ideal. We\'ve designed a "Generate Preview" button and infrastructure scaling to fix this.

🔧

Assembly Experience

Most customers wanted pre-assembled options. This directly led to our Basic + Frame and Premium Product offerings.

🎨

Customisation Demand

Customers want more: coloured water layers, font choices, accessories, epoxy finishes. These feed directly into our product roadmap.

🏪

Shopify Performance

Zero issues reported with the Shopify storefront, checkout, or order management. A reliable foundation for scaling.

📦

Fragile Parts Risk

Some maps had very thin roads that could break. We\'ve since introduced minimum feature width guards in the blueprint builder.

The Beta Made Us Confident

Our beta trial proved the concept, validated the technology, and gave us a clear roadmap. We're now ready for commercial launch — with a better product, a proven platform, and real customer insights.
