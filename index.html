About Map-Art Store

Changing the Way Personalised Maps Are Made

Map-Art Store is transforming custom home and office décor by allowing buyers to see exactly what their personalised product will look like — in real time. Our proprietary technology moves the buying experience from a slow, high-touch process to a fast, self-serve journey.

"To bridge distances by encapsulating memories through intricately designed, personalised geographical engravings, fostering a connection between people and places."

Founded and headquartered in Winnipeg, Manitoba, we are proud to create jobs, invest in the local economy, and hold all our intellectual property in Canada.
